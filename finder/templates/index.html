Represent

The open database of Canadian elected representatives and electoral districts

Represent, by Open North, is a database and web service that maps postal codes and points to current administrative boundaries and elected representatives at the federal, provincial and municipal levels of government in Canada.

Submit

Coverage

Represent is an open database of:

  • Members of Parliament (MP)
  • Members of the Legislative Assembly (MLA)
  • Members of the Provincial Parliament (MPP)
  • Members of the National Assembly (MNA)
  • mayors and city councillors
  • Federal electoral districts
  • Provincial electoral districts
  • Census subdivisions
  • Municipal boundaries
  • Ward and borough boundaries

It has both representatives and boundaries for:

  • Canada
  • Alberta
  • British Columbia
  • Manitoba
  • New Brunswick
  • Newfoundland and Labrador
  • Nova Scotia
