    Represent

    The open database of Canadian elected representatives and electoral districts

    Represent, by Open North, is a database and web service that maps postal codes and points to current administrative boundaries and elected representatives at the federal, provincial and municipal levels of government in Canada.

    Coverage

    Represent is an open database of:

    • Members of Parliament (MP)
    • Members of the Legislative Assembly (MLA)
    • Members of the Provincial Parliament (MPP)
    • Members of the National Assembly (MNA)
    • mayors and city councillors
    • Federal electoral districts
    • Provincial electoral districts
    • Census subdivisions
    • Municipal boundaries
    • Ward and borough boundaries

    Contributing

    Represent was built by volunteers. You can help make it better.

    If you’ve got digital boundary files (“shapefiles”) for districts we don’t have, please let us know.

    Our representative data comes from a gaggle of web scrapers. If you can write code in Python, Ruby, PHP, or JavaScript, you can add a new scraper or improve our existing ones. Learn how.

    Represent is open source under the MIT license. The digital boundary files are available under various licenses. Please consult the API for information on data sources.

    Represent was built by Michael Mulley and James McKinney. We took inspiration and code from the Chicago Tribune’s Boundary Service.

    Important contributions were made by Aran Rasmussen, Kent Mewhort, Amir Pakdel, Camilo Lopez, Rory Geoghegan, Trevor Knight, and Carmine Casciato.
