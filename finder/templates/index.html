    Represent

    The open database of Canadian elected representatives and electoral districts

    Represent, by Open North, is a database and web service that maps postal codes and points to current administrative boundaries and elected representatives at the federal, provincial and municipal levels of government in Canada.

    Submit

    Coverage

    Represent is an open database of:

    • Members of Parliament (MP)
    • Members of the Legislative Assembly (MLA)
    • Members of the Provincial Parliament (MPP)
    • Members of the National Assembly (MNA)
    • mayors and city councillors
    • Federal electoral districts
    • Provincial electoral districts
    • Census subdivisions
    • Municipal boundaries
    • Ward and borough boundaries

    Usage and API

    Represent establishes a foundation upon which other projects may be built - a necessary piece of infrastructure which we hope will enable useful and innovative features and products. Please consult the API documentation on how to use this web service and subscribe to our mailing list to stay up-to-date on API and data changes.

    Contributing

    Represent was built by volunteers. You can help make it better.

    • Add to our list of scrapers to get the elected officials for more cities (find out more)
    • Ask provincial Municipal Affairs ministries for municipal elected officials in Excel format
    • Ask municipalities to provide their ward boundaries under an open license
    • Donate to Open North so a staff member can improve and maintain this service part-time

    That's our top four, and there are many more ways in which you can help. Contact us if you're interested or join our mailing list.
